Our Manifesto.

  • The Awakening

    The mission of UTM is to inspire the younger generations through the stories of success of today’s native leaders, masterminds and trend-setters.

    "I am not a teacher, but an awakener.”

    – Robert Frost, Poet

  • The Unlocking

    Our decoded books are providing Millennials and Generation Z direct access to first-hand knowledge from the present country's movers and shakers.

    “Mentors change lives, but students change mentors’ lives more.”

    – Richie Norton, Inspirational speaker

  • The Approaching

    Our approach is quite pure and straight-forward: inspiration through personal storytelling, direct/indirect talks and personal mentorship.

    "If I hadn't had mentors, I wouldn't be here today."

    – Indra Nooyi, CEO PepsiCo

  • The Mentoring

    Company culture, CSR, engagement, purpose, equity and inclusion are some of the values that are promoted among our profiled compilations.

    “Find a mentor who has skills in the area you want to develop. That sounds so simple, but it’s the key to a successful relationship.”

    – Erin Lynn, Writer

  • The Learning

    UTM is paving the way to a new intergenerational knowledge, understanding and expertise, executed through our VIP tale-telling book collection.

    “Children must be taught how to think, not what to think.”

    – Margaret Mead, Anthropologist

  • The Connecting

    We conduct one-on-one interviews, assemble them with inspiring narratives and donate the books – together with our partners – to organizations.

    "If you cannot see where you are going, ask someone who has been there before."

    – J. Loren Norris, Motivational coach

Native Role Models
vs Globalisation.

Globalisation is here to stay – and we believe in its priceless and numerous advantages; however we cannot forget the inner truth that connects every and single one of the individuals of a state, country or nation.

The older generations used to pass their experiences in small gatherings, many times around a secretive bonfire or a warm cup of tea. Nowadays, UTM is performing as the most effective conductive thread in between generations of leaders and achievers.

Inspiration as
a Way of Life.

Our books are meant to be touched, passed on from one hand to another, read alone or in working groups, quoted in college classes, utilized in executive meetings or roundtables, introduced at all levels of the public sphere, etc.

As we ultimately close the circle, our country-publications should be the ultimate winning-ticket for those wandering young minds who are seeking direct/indirect talks and mentorship from their much admired countrymen and women.

Together
We Achieve.

And how is it done? We conduct 60 to 75 face-to-face interviews with our VIP personalities, compile them together with inspiring narratives and give away the books free of charge – along with the invaluable support of our partners and donors – to universities, business schools, non-profit social and community development foundations, think tanks, libraries, governmental offices, selected private institutions and beyond.

The Book.

We are currently producing two UTM editions: Qatar and Spain.

Qatar is mesmerizing the world with its leadership, forward-thinking knowledge-based economy and productive change for the world and region. Spain, at the vanguard of social rights, entrepreneurship and disruptive technologies, is piloting Europe onto the next level of development and community welfare.
